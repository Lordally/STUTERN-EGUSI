Egusi Soup

Introduction

This is a special soup that makes you lick and even chew your fingers. It’s off the charts for effort vs output: just a handful of everyday spices, Onion, a cup of grinded raw egusi, crayfish, ugwu leaf, fish and meat.

When this egusi is cooking, the aroma is very amazing and Incredible!!! To know more about Egusi recipe!

How to Serve Egusi Soup

There are two main ways to serve Egusi Soup.

  1. As Egusi Soup – with Fufu, Chicken and a bottle of coke on the side.
  2. And the other way is with Eba, Meat and a bottle of chilled water on the side.

Side Dishes

A few things that would be very much at home alongside Egusi Soup:

  • Juice
  • Pounded Yam
  • Chilled Soft drinks

Ingredients

Item Unit Measurement
Egusi 1 kg
Fish 6 pcs
Salt 1 tbsp
Meat 1 cup
crayfish 2 tbsp
